About Nature Knitted

Nature Knitted is about combining two loves into unique creations that celebrate the outdoors and the easily overlooked creatures we share this earth with. From tiny insects to mountain landscapes, the outdoors has something for everyone to enjoy. From machine embroidery to iron on vinyl we have the ability to create unique creations and would love to work with you to make your own custom creations.
